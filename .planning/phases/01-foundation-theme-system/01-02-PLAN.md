    1. Create `src/styles/textures.css` with CSS/SVG-only BSD visual motifs (no external images):

       a. Paper noise texture (`.texture-paper`): Uses ::before pseudo-element with inline SVG feTurbulence filter (fractalNoise, baseFrequency 0.65, numOctaves 3), low opacity (0.03-0.05), absolute positioned overlay with pointer-events: none.

       b. Bandage stripe pattern (`.texture-bandage`): CSS repeating-linear-gradient at 45deg, alternating transparent and faint parchment-colored stripes (4px transparent, 1px rgba parchment at 0.05 opacity).

       c. Classified stamp (`.stamp-classified`): Red border (3px solid classified-red), uppercase monospace text, letter-spacing 0.2em, slight rotation (-5deg), reduced opacity (0.8).

       d. Ink splatter accent (`.texture-ink`): CSS radial-gradient with irregular shapes simulating brush strokes. Multiple layered radial gradients at different positions with very low opacity.

       e. Aged paper edges (`.texture-aged-edge`): Box shadow with inset shadows in dark tones to simulate worn edges on card elements.

       f. Ability glow effect (`.glow-ability`): Box-shadow with teal color spread, plus a subtle animation keyframe for pulse effect. Respects prefers-reduced-motion.
